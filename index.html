Oscar Billing

Historical Linguist, Piece of Shit, Lover

I am a historical linguist and philologist, currently active as a PhD Candidate of Indo-European linguistics at Uppsala University. My project is an investigation into the accent systems of the Luwic languages in both synchrony and diachrony. I am generally interested in all things relating to language and language change, not least including reconstruction, phylogenetics, and the phonological decipherment of complex writing systems.

On this website, you may find my publications and other things about me. I am always open to new acquaintances and new ideas, so please do not hesitate to contact me if you can offer any of those things.
